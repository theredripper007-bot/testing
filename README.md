# NUMBER MEDICINE
### Feel · Form · Move · Sky

> *What does this form ask me to feel — and how does it ask me to move?*  
> *Mapped eight · then beyond: Universe · the Unknown*

Nine living forms. Each carries **four layers**:

1. **Feel** — emotional medicine (LOVE, SHARING, DESIRE…)  
2. **Form** — geometric kinetic map (line, curve, open loop, cross, spiral…)  
3. **Move** — smallest body instruction  
4. **Sky** — mapped eight (Sun…Neptune) or **beyond** (Universe · the Unknown)

---

## Sky structure (honest)

| Forms | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **1 – 8** | **Mapped eight** — Sun · Mercury · Venus · Mars · Earth · Jupiter · Saturn · Neptune |
| **9** | **Beyond the eight** — **Universe · the Unknown** (never a planet) |

There are **eight** officially recognized planets. Form nine is contemplative language for the whole and the uncharted — not a sky catalog entry. The scientific “Planet Nine” hypothesis is separate and unobserved.

**Site law:** only digits **1–9** as numerals on the public UI (no empty-mark, no counts beyond nine).
